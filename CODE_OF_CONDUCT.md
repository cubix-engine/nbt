As members of this community, we pledge to make participation in our projects a safe, welcoming, and respectful experience for everyone.

## Rules
- **Be respectful** — Our team includes people of all ages, nationalities, races, and religions. Slurs, targeted insults, and advocacy of harm are not tolerated.
- **Keep it appropriate** — Gore, sexual content, and other explicit material are strictly prohibited.
- **Respect privacy** — Do not share personal or private information without consent.
- **Communicate constructively** — Feedback and discussions should aim to help the project and each other.
- **No disruptive behavior** — Avoid spam, trolling, or actions that negatively impact the community

## Scope
These guidelines apply to all community spaces and participants, including but not limited to:
- Code repositories (e.g., issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Chat platforms (e.g., Discord, forums)
- Any other official or affiliated spaces

---

## Enforcement
We may not be able to monitor all activity at all times, but reports are encouraged.

Violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from the community, depending on severity.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it through the appropriate contact channels for this community.

---

By participating, you agree to follow these guidelines and contribute to a positive and inclusive environment.
